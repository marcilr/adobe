Summary
-------
Join us for the three-part Adobe Document Cloud Skill Builder series.
Each one-hour session will give you task-based tools and tips to help
you go digital, stay digital, and get work done.  You'll learn how to
use Adobe Acrobat DC and mobile apps (including Adobe Scan and Adobe
Acrobat Reader), and how to perform basic but powerful document tasks
that simplify your job. Attend all three sessions and the Adobe team
will send you a certificate of completion to acknowledge your
participation in the Skill Builder series
